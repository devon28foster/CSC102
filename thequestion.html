You end up on a deserted island, you only have a back scratcher, 2 jars of mayonaise, and a lunch bag. When you arrive you notice the island smells like rotten mangos. As you are exploring the island a swarm of hummingbirds appears and disappears from your view.You start to realize that they are following you in a pattern. They start getting closer and closer. As you are running from then you find a secret passage behind some vines that leads to a tower in a empty clearing surrounded by mountains. You approach the tower and look for a door. After you push on a brick you start to notice a sound. Then you realize that the clearing you are in starts to fill up with water. You go back to the vines but cannot find the entry and water is fillng fast. You are forced to start climbing up the side of the rock wall. What is the best word in the english language?
